I am very happy to announce that WildFly 39.0.1.Final is now released and available link:/downloads[for download] (or to be provisioned by https://docs.wildfly.org/39/Galleon_Guide.html[Galleon], fetched as https://quay.io/repository/wildfly/wildfly[Container images on Quay], or obtained by whichever distribution mechanism you prefer).

The complete WildFly 39.0.1.Final release notes are link:https://github.com/wildfly/wildfly/releases/tag/39.0.1.Final[available on GitHub, window=_blank].

I want to highlight great work of our community users during this maintenance release.
Rhuan H. found a memory leak https://issues.redhat.com/browse/WFLY-21390[issue] and did absolutely amazing work analyzing, debugging and verifying the fix.
Cody L. found and promptly https://issues.redhat.com/browse/WFLY-21383[reported] an issue where `max-post-size` in Undertow was not being properly applied,
capping uploads at 2 MB.

Give it a try, upgrade and give us your feedback, in the link:https://groups.google.com/g/wildfly[WildFly google group, window=_blank], link:https://wildfly.zulipchat.com/#narrow/stream/196266-wildfly-user[Zulip, window=_blank] or link:https://issues.redhat.com/projects/WFLY/summary[Jira, window=_blank].
